Understanding Our Business Advances

A QuickCap Ltd Business Advance is a straightforward way to access working capital. Unlike traditional loans, it's an advance on your future sales. We provide a lump sum, and you repay it via small, agreed-upon daily or weekly payments that align with your business's cash flow. There are no hidden fees, and the total cost is agreed upfront.
